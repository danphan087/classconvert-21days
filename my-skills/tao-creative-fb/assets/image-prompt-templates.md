# Image Prompt Templates cho tao-creative-fb

## Cấu trúc chung cho Prompt
Để hình ảnh chân thực, sáng sủa và đúng chuẩn "Stock Photo" như ảnh bạn tham khảo, prompt gửi cho DALL-E 3 phải sử dụng cấu trúc:
`A bright, high-quality stock photo of a young Vietnamese teacher in a modern, well-lit classroom. [MÔ TẢ HÀNH ĐỘNG/TRANG PHỤC]. Soft natural daylight, high-key lighting, shallow depth of field with blurred students in the background. Professional yet approachable, pure real-life photography, 8k resolution, no digital art.`

## Mode 1: Organic Post (Chia sẻ giá trị, cuộc sống)
**Mục tiêu:** Ảnh lớp học thực tế, giáo viên đang tương tác tự nhiên, phong cách stock photo sáng sủa.
**Template mẫu:**
"A bright, high-quality stock photo of a smiling Vietnamese teacher wearing a professional blazer, standing in a well-lit modern classroom holding a tablet. Soft natural daylight from large windows. Shallow depth of field with softly blurred students sitting in the foreground. High-key lighting, pure real-life photography, 8k."

## Mode 2: Creative Ads (Quảng cáo chuyển đổi)
**Mục tiêu:** Ảnh chụp sáng, chuyên nghiệp, biểu cảm chân thực, chừa không gian để chèn chữ.

**Angle 1 (Pain Point):**
"A bright, high-quality stock photo of a Vietnamese teacher sitting at a desk in a classroom, looking slightly tired while checking a laptop. Even with bright natural daylight, the exhaustion of manual work is visible. Professional attire. Leave negative space on the left side. Pure real-life photography."

**Angle 2 (Solution/Benefit):**
"A bright, cheerful high-quality stock photo of a Vietnamese teacher in a modern classroom, holding a clipboard and looking very happy and relieved while teaching. Soft natural daylight, high-key lighting, shallow depth of field with a whiteboard in the background. Leave empty space at the top. Pure photography."

**Angle 3 (Social Proof):**
"A bright, high-quality stock photo of a Vietnamese teacher standing in a classroom, warmly interacting and explaining something to a student. Both are smiling. Soft natural daylight, shallow depth of field, professional and trustworthy atmosphere. Pure real-life photography, 8k."
